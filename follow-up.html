Tooling for composites

DUTCH-SHAPE IS ONE OF THE WORLD'S LEADING SUPPLIERS OF TOOLING FOR LARGE, COMPLEX COMPOSITE PARTS AND ASSEMBLIES.

We have developed several tooling solutions especially for composite products. We design the tooling optimizing for the heat-up/cool down rates, spring back behaviour, the accuracy and weight.

Tools are made from INVAR or if possible from less expensive materials (Steel, Aluminium, Carbon). They are milled out of billets or from formed sheets with egg-box frames. So we can guarantee a minimal variation in wall thickness, to optimize the thermal behaviour of the tool.
